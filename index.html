Gameplay

Our gameplay

Project Anime gameplay is all about hacking and slashing your enemies to bits! You play as either Asahina Yuuki, Kabuto Kirigaya or Lightsaber, our protagonists of the game. Developed by the returning developer of the award-winning game Genesis for the PC/PS4/XboxOne, this game features the non-stop sword thrusting, slashing, kill-em-all action gameplay that all of our fans loved dearly. Play as a lone swordmaster in the post-apocalyptic world by yourself or team up with your friends for the juicy, mutants destroying action or if that's not the right cup of tea for you then play against other players in the team deathmatch mode!

Lightsaber

Gender: ???
Weight: 42 kg
Height: 154 cm
Age: ???
Occupation: Legendary Sword
Class: Servant

Lightsaber is a legendary sword that only a true swordmaster can wield. Certain phenomena in the world (likely the one that caused Asahina and Kabuto to wake up and find themselves in this world) caused the legendary sword to take up the form of a human and fight its enemies. It later encounters Asahina and Kabuto and joins them on their journey. It can also take the form of the sword again and Kabuto is able to wield it.

Kabuto Kirigaya

Gender: Male
Weight: 59 kg
Height: 172 cm
Age: 17
Occupation: High School Student, Part-Time Tester
Class: Dual Sword Wielder

Kabuto Kirigaya is Asahina Yuuki's boyfriend and future husband. As if he is connected to Asahina Yuuki by fate, when she wakes up to find herself in real life, so does Kabuto Kirigaya. Kabuto Kirigaya is a very avid gamer and takes every game and match he plays very competitively. Some people may even accuse him of cheating, he is just that good. Gaming also got him to want to be a part-time tester for a project that will revolutionize gaming as we know it.

Asahina Yuuki

Gender: Female
Weight: ???
Height: ???
Age: 18
Occupation: High School Student
Class: Swordmaster

Asahina Yuuki is one of the playable characters in project anime. Originally a character of a game, one day she wakes up and finds herself in another world which is apparently the real life. Her personality is cheerful and she is a very good leader. Her skills as a swordmaster are unmatched by her rivals. She is very popular and is always surrounded by her group of friends. Her boyfriend Kabuto Kirigaya also happens to be summoned to this world.
